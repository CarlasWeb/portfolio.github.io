CVP

CVP is one of Europe's leading Broadcast and Professional video equipment providers, with an extensive eCommerce website.

My work at CVP

Responsive design and build

In today's digital world, responsive design and development play a pivotal role, ensuring seamless compatibility across all devices to improve user experience, accessibility, and navigational ease, also it significantly enhances visibility on search engines, amplifying online presence and engagement.

My main goal is to ensure that every page looks fantastic and functions smoothly on any device. I achieve this by using CSS tools like flexbox, grid, media queries, and fluid images. Whether someone is browsing on a computer, tablet, or phone, I want them to have a seamless experience. My aim is to create web pages that are user-friendly and visually appealing, regardless of how visitors access them.

The job

In my role as the front-end developer at CVP, I play a crucial role in the ongoing maintenance and enhancement of their extensive e-commerce platform. My responsibilities include a wide array of tasks aimed at ensuring the website remains modern, functional, and user-friendly.

One of my main duties involves the creation and upkeep of front-end templates using a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These templates serve as the foundation for the website's layout, design elements, and interactive features, providing visitors with a seamless browsing experience.

I also have involvement in the development of internal systems used within the company, using front-end technologies alongside backend solutions such as PHP and MySQL.

In addition to website development, I am tasked with coding HTML email templates for various purposes, including order confirmations and promotional offers.
