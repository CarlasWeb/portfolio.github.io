About Me

Hello, I'm Carla Arrowsmith, and I'm passionate about the world of web development. With a career spanning since June 2021, I've been fortunate to work as a junior front-end web developer at CVP, a prominent leader in broadcast and professional video solutions throughout the UK and Europe.

My journey at CVP has been a rewarding one, I actively contribute to the development and enhancement of several key projects. Among these, I have been a crucial part of the team responsible for the maintenance and improvement of their expansive e-commerce website, ensuring it provides a seamless and user-friendly experience to customers. Additionally, I have played a pivotal role in crafting the online presence of CVP's London Studio, helping to showcase their creative offerings to a wider audience. Beyond these external-facing projects, I've also engaged with internal systems, refining and streamlining processes for enhanced efficiency.

Throughout my tenure, I have had the opportunity to hone my skills in various web technologies. My proficiency in HTML and CSS has grown significantly, enabling me to create visually appealing and responsive web interfaces. Moreover, I've developed a solid foundation in JavaScript and PHP, which has allowed me to add dynamic elements to websites and contribute to the functionality of CVP's digital ecosystem.

My time at CVP has been a rich learning experience, and I'm excited to continue my journey predominantly in front-end web development, exploring new technologies and techniques to create engaging and impactful online experiences.
