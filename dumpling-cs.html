London Dumpling

London Dumpling is a template website, designed and built to demonstrate best practices in modern web design and development. It serves as an example of a fully responsive and visually appealing website that could be adapted for a wide range of businesses or projects, such as restaurants, cafes, or food-related ventures.

My work at London Dumpling

Responsive design and build

Responsive design and development are essential for creating websites and applications that function seamlessly across all devices. With users accessing content from desktops, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring compatibility across various screen sizes is crucial. Responsive design not only enhances user experience and accessibility but also improves navigation and usability. Furthermore, it boosts search engine rankings, helping to increase online visibility and engagement by making websites mobile-friendly and optimized for search engines.

My primary goal is to build web pages that look great and perform flawlessly, regardless of the device being used. By leveraging CSS techniques like flexbox, grid, media queries, and fluid images, I ensure that layouts adapt effortlessly to different screen sizes. Whether users are browsing on a computer, tablet, or phone, my aim is to deliver a visually appealing, user-friendly experience that works consistently across all platforms.

The job

I created London Dumpling as a template website to utilise various skills. Taking charge of both design and development, I crafted the logo and website layout using Figma. Building upon this foundation, I used HTML, CSS, Vue.js, Swiper.js and Bootstrap to bring the website to life.
